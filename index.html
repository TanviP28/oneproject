What is OneSevenBio?

We are dedicated to transforming the way you shop and live sustainably. Our platform hosts an extensive database of eco-conscious products sourced locally. Whether you're seeking sustainable fashion, or zero-waste home essentials, we curate options that align with YOUR VIBE.

Who are the Founders?

The Founders of OneSevenBio are Flaviya Shibu, Shagun Soni and Aashi Agarwal. They are incredibly passionate about sustainability, social enterprise, and sarcasm. Their laziness to look for sustainable options on the internet persuaded them to come up with the idea. So, if you're lazy about surfing the internet for credible shopping brands but passionate about sustainability, sign up to join our Community.

Why are we doing this?

With OneSevenBio, we aim to reward you for your laziness. How? We aim to provide you with a plethora of sustainable and quality brand options in various categories like clothing, bags, shoes, and many more. But this won't be restricted to just knowing the brand's existence; it also encompasses learning about the background, founders, sustainability processes, and other measures taken by the brand through which you can choose if you really want to buy from their website or not. So basically, long story short – we're giving you a database of sustainable companies customized as per your needs, providing you with all the information you might need to choose if it is really worth your hard-earned money and also rewarding you with exciting offers and gifts while doing it. Sounds fun, doesn't it!
